Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

SolarWriter acts as a hub between your keyboard and your e-reader. You type on the keyboard, and the text appears on both on the iOS device and on the e-reader's screen in a web browser.

Why can't I just connect a keyboard directly to my Kindle?

The Kindle doesn't have the bluetooth hardware necessary to connect a keyboard. SolarWriter uses your iOS device's hardware to overcome this limitation.

How do I set it up?

Up-to-date instructions are included on the Help page inside the app. Basically you must connect your bluetooth keyboard to your iOS device, connect your iOS device and e-reader to the same WiFi network (possibly your iPhone's Personal Hotspot), and visit a web address given in the app (no Internet connection required) in your e-reader's browser.

Does it work with all e-readers or just the Kindle?

SolarWriter works with any e-reader that has a web browser. Try the app out for free before purchasing to be sure it works for you.

Does it work with the iPhone and iPad?

Yes, SolarWriter works on any iOS device. To work outside without a Wifi network, an iPhone with a plan supporting Personal Hotspot is recommended.

Is the screen update rate noticable?

The screen updates as fast as the e-reader hardware can. On a Kindle PaperWhite or Voyage, the delay is noticable but easy to work with after just a few moments of typing. The best way to find out is to try SolarWriter yourself.

What keyboards are supported?

SolarWriter uses the native bluetooth keyboard connection on your iOS device, and will therefore work with any keyboard that works with iOS. SolarWriter was tested primarily with an Apple bluetooth keyboard.
